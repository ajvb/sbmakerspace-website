Interested in Volunteering?

The Santa Barbara Public Library System is partnering with Santa Barbara Makerspace to offer classes and open nights to provide opportunities for makers and developers of all ages. If you would like to teach, mentor, donate, or sponsor tools, equipment and/or supplies to support the Library in this effort, please tell us your email. We will send you updates to tell you how you can help.
